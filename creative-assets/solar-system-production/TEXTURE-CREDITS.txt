Texture setup for this project
==============================

Place your texture files inside:
assets/textures/

Expected texture filenames:
sun.webp
mercury.webp
venus-surface.webp
venus-atmosphere.webp
earth-day.webp
earth-night.webp
earth-clouds.webp
earth-specular-map.webp
moon.webp
mars.webp
jupiter.webp
saturn.webp
saturn-ring.webp
uranus.webp
neptune.webp
stars.webp
stars-milky-way.webp

Expected normal-map filenames:
assets/normal-maps/earth-normal.webp
assets/normal-maps/mars-normal.webp
assets/normal-maps/moon-normal.webp

If your downloaded file names differ, either rename your files to match the list above or edit js/sss-planets.js.

Recommended texture source:
Solar System Scope Textures
https://www.solarsystemscope.com/textures/

Check and follow the texture licence/usage terms from the source you downloaded from.
