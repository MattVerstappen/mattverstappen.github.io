## About

Cafe styled gameplay experience made for Ludum Dare 56. Players manage a tiny cafe, serving customers and keeping up with orders in a charming point-and-click format.

Ludum Dare 56 is one of the world's largest global game jams, with thousands of entrants competing within a 72-hour window. Placing Top 500 out of the full global field is a strong competitive result.
