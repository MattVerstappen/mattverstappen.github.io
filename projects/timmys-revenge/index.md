## About

Timmy's Revenge is a top-down 2D shooter where players battle relentless waves of zombies, unlocking powerful new weapons as they face the undead horde. Developed for a Game Jam, this roguelike game challenges players to control Timmy, strategically fighting off enemies and surviving increasingly difficult waves.
