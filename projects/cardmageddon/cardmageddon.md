## About

Thrown into a deep dungeon and set in the Mighty Maze, explore and embark on an epic adventure to become the Card Mage. Scattered around the maze, collect a myriad of card-based summons to use along your journey - but their use comes at a great cost. Strategy must always be considered if you wish to triumph and win back your freedom by collecting every card.
