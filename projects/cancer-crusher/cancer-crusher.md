## About

A 2D fixed shooter using retro-styled gameplay where you journey through the body to kill cancer. Players fight through 3 increasingly corrupted levels, each featuring 3 waves and a boss fight, raising awareness of testicular cancer in partnership with Love Your Nuts.

Cancer Crusher was developed in partnership with Love Your Nuts, a Section 18A NPO focused on testicular cancer awareness among adolescents. The game uses accessible, retro-inspired gameplay to deliver a serious health education message to a younger audience.
