## About

This business plan outlines the commercial strategy for Atlas Games (Pty) Ltd, a South African impact-first indie game studio developing culturally localised, curriculum-aligned serious games for health education.

### Founding Product

**Cancer Crusher** — created in partnership with Love Your Nuts, a Section 18A NPO focused on testicular cancer awareness among adolescents.

### Revenue Model

Hybrid B2B model with six income streams:

1. Impact services
2. Library licensing (school and institutional)
3. Corporate CSR sponsorship
4. Support SLAs
5. Teacher training programmes
6. Non-dilutive grant funding

**Year 1 Projected Revenue:** R1,200,000

### Growth Strategy

Phased six-year scaling plan:

- **Phase 1 (Year 1–2):** Provincial pilot — Cancer Crusher in KwaZulu-Natal schools
- **Phase 2 (Year 2–3):** Multi-region rollout across South Africa
- **Phase 3 (Year 3–4):** Teacher dashboards and LMS integration
- **Phase 4 (Year 4–5):** Channel partnerships with NGOs and government health departments
- **Phase 5 (Year 5–6):** Pan-African NGO collaboration and regional expansion

### Risk Management

Eight key risks identified with pre-committed mitigation strategies. Quarterly KPI reviews covering:
- Commercial performance
- Product stability
- Measurable learning impact
